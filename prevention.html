✅ How to Avoid QR Code Attacks

  • ✔️ Always preview the URL before opening it after scanning.
  • ✔️ Avoid scanning random or suspicious QR codes in public places.
  • ✔️ Use trusted QR scanner apps with security filters.
  • ✔️ Never enter personal info on unknown websites opened from QR codes.
  • ✔️ Educate friends and coworkers about QR-based phishing.
